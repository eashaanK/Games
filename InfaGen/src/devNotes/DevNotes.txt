InfaGen

Infa Gen is an RPG Based 2D game. The player is one character that is spawned on a randomly generated "infinite" world. The objective of the game is to
explore your world as far as the cpu would allow you. You can grow crops, eat, drink, craft, tame wild animals, destroy trees and stones to build 
houses and other structures. In the night, there will be enemies that come after you.

- Basic infinite tiled terrain. The blocks are very small, but there's are lot of them. 
- The player always stays in the center
- There is some basic lighting
- Collision physics
- Screen changing modes to enter in and out of structures
